The Best Movies According to Kent

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

A story of a young girl who was trapped in a mysterious place. She embarks on a journey to find a way back to her own world, and to do so, she must adapt and overcome challenges while displaying resilience and determination.

Your Name

It is a story of two high school students,Mitsuha and Taki, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies with a different timelines, despite never having met.

Weathering with You

A romantic film where a high school boy, Hodaka Morishima, runs away from his troubled rural home to Tokyo. There, he befriends Hina Amano, an orphaned girl who has the ability to control the weather. This film explores their relationship and the impact of Hina's mysterious power.
